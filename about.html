About Chai Tales

Brewing Stories, One Cup at a Time

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Chai Tales is more than just a café — it’s where every sip tells a story. Rooted in the rich tradition of Indian chai culture, we bring together timeless recipes and modern flavors to create a tea experience like no other. From the creamy comfort of milk chai to the boldness of black chai and the refreshing zest of herbal blends, our menu is crafted to suit every mood and moment.

We believe chai is not just a drink — it’s a ritual of togetherness, conversations, and memories. Every cup we serve is brewed with care, using authentic methods and ingredients that preserve the essence of age-old traditions. With every sip, we aim to deliver not just flavor, but also comfort, warmth, and connection.

Our space is designed to feel like home — a place where friends can catch up, families can share moments, and strangers can become companions over steaming cups of chai. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick break, a long evening of laughter, or some quiet time to unwind, Chai Tales is the perfect setting to relax and recharge.

At the heart of our journey are our values — authenticity, hospitality, and sustainability. We honor the true spirit of chai while embracing creativity to introduce unique blends. We’re committed to supporting local farmers and eco-friendly practices, giving back to the community while serving you with heartfelt warmth.

More than a restaurant, Chai Tales is an experience — a place where stories are shared, bonds are strengthened, and every visit feels like a warm embrace. Step into our world, take a sip, and let us be a part of your story, one cup at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Chai Tales special?

At Chai Tales, we combine authentic Indian chai traditions with modern flavors, creating a unique tea experience that feels both nostalgic and refreshing.

Do you only serve chai?

While chai is our specialty, we also serve herbal teas, light snacks, and treats that perfectly complement your cup of tea.

Are your ingredients natural?

Yes! We use carefully sourced tea leaves, fresh spices, and locally sourced ingredients to ensure quality, freshness, and sustainability.

Can I work or study at Chai Tales?

Absolutely! Our cozy space is designed for comfort, whether you’re here for conversations, work, or simply to unwind with a warm cup.

Do you offer dairy-free or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a variety of dairy-free and vegan alternatives, including plant-based milk options, so everyone can enjoy their favorite chai.

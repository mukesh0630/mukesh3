About CT Cafè

CT Cafè was founded in 2016. Welcome to Chai Tales — where every sip tells a story.

Born from a love of soulful conversations and the timeless charm of chai, Chai Tales is more than just a tea spot — it’s an experience. Whether you're catching up with old friends, diving into your favorite book, or just taking a break from the chaos of the day, we’ve brewed the perfect atmosphere for you.

From the rich aroma of masala chai to our handcrafted snacks inspired by street-side favorites, every item on our menu is steeped in tradition and served with a modern twist. Cozy corners, warm vibes, and the comforting clink of chai glasses — it’s all waiting for you here.

Come for the chai, stay for the tales.
